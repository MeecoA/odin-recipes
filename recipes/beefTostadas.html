Crispy Oven Beef-and-Bean Tostadas

Description

A healthier way to make tostadas and better tasting. The simple secret to making crispy tostadas is baking the corn tortillas instead of frying.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Beef
  • 1 1/2 kgBeans
  • 1 pack Taco
  • 2 Onions
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the needed Ingredients, cut the onions, wash the beans as well as the beef
  2. Heat up the taco in the oven
  3. Cook the beans and the beef to your desired doneness. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. After cooking, combine all the Ingredients and enjoy!
